# Project Overview
We are building a custom operations management platform for a vacation rental cleaning company called Indigo Harts Cleaning Service.
The platform will consist of:
1 A React Native mobile application (iOS and Android)
2 A Next.js web administration portal
3 A Supabase backend for authentication, database, storage, and security
4 Twilio integration for SMS messaging

⠀This is a Phase 1 MVP intended to streamline cleaning operations, employee scheduling, guest communications, and property management.

# Core Business Model
The company cleans short-term rental properties.
Each property may have:
* Photos
* Access codes
* Cleaning instructions
* Guest instructions
* SMS templates
* Cleaning checklist templates

⠀Properties receive guest stays.
Guests check in and check out.
Cleaning jobs are scheduled between stays.
Employees complete cleaning jobs and notify administrators when complete.
